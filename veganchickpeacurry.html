Vegan Chickpea Curry

What is Vegan Chickpea Curry

Vegan Chickpea Curry is a flavorful and nutritious plant-based dish. It combines chickpeas with aromatic spices, coconut milk, and vegetables. Simmered to perfection, it offers a creamy, hearty texture and a rich, savory taste. It's a popular vegan-friendly choice for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

Ingredient List For Vegan Chickpea Curry Recipe

  • Chickpeas (canned or cooked)
  • Coconut milk
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Curry powder
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Chili powder (optional, for heat)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (for garnish)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon juice (optional, for extra tanginess)

How to make Vegan Chickpea Curry

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.

2. Add finely chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger to the pan. Saut√© until the onion turns translucent and the mixture becomes aromatic.

3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder (if using). Cook for a minute to toast the spices.

4. Add diced tomatoes and cook until they soften and break down, forming a thick tomato sauce.

5. Pour in coconut milk, stirring well to combine with the tomato mixture. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

6. Add cooked or canned chickpeas (drained and rinsed) to the pan. Stir to coat them evenly with the curry sauce.

7. Season the curry with salt and black pepper to taste. Adjust the spiciness and seasoning as needed.

8. Let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the chickpeas to absorb the sauce. If the curry thickens too much, you can add a bit of water to reach your desired consistency.

9. Finish the Vegan Chickpea Curry by adding a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for extra tanginess. Adjust the lemon juice to your taste preference.

10. Garnish the curry with fresh cilantro leaves.

11. Serve the Vegan Chickpea Curry hot, either with rice, quinoa, or naan bread, for a delicious and satisfying plant-based meal.
